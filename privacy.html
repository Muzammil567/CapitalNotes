3. Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies to enhance your gaming experience and remember your preferences. We also work with advertising partners, including Google AdSense, to display relevant advertisements. For more detailed information about how we use cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

3.1 Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Show personalized ads based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent the same ads from being shown too many times
  • Ensure ads are properly displayed

3.2 Your Advertising Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3.3 Google AdSense Implementation

Our website implements Google AdSense advertisements in the following ways:

  • Display ads: Banner advertisements appear in designated areas of our website
  • Responsive ads: Advertisements adapt to your screen size and device type
  • In-game ads: Advertisements may appear before, during, or after gameplay

3.4 Data Collection for Advertising

Through our advertising partners, particularly Google AdSense, the following information may be collected:

  • Device information (type, model, operating system)
  • Browser type and settings
  • IP address and location data
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Game interaction and preferences

This information is used to provide you with more relevant advertisements and improve your gaming experience. We do not directly collect or store this advertising-related data; it is managed by our advertising partners in accordance with their privacy policies.
