Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CapitalNotes ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using our website and games, including:

  • Information you provide when contacting us
  • Game preferences and settings
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Device information and browser type

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Communicate with you about updates and new features
  • Ensure the security of our platform

3. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your gaming experience and remember your preferences. For more detailed information about how we use cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure data storage practices

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes become effective immediately upon posting.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@capitalnotes.com or Contact Us.
