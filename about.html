Content Guidelines

At CapitalNotes, we maintain high standards for our educational content:

  • All games are designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages
  • Financial concepts are simplified without compromising accuracy
  • Content is regularly reviewed and updated by financial education experts
  • Games are tested for educational value and engagement
  • User feedback is incorporated into content improvements

Editorial Policies

Our commitment to quality educational content is guided by these principles:

  • Accuracy: All financial information is thoroughly researched and verified
  • Transparency: Sources of information are clearly cited where applicable
  • Updates: Content is regularly reviewed and updated to reflect current financial practices
  • Accessibility: Information is presented in clear, understandable language
  • Feedback: We welcome and incorporate user suggestions for improvement
